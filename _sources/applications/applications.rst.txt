Applications
============

.. include:: /_course_toc.rst

The **Five-Star Battery Data** framework is not only a set of principlesâ€”it is a practical approach that can be applied across the full battery research and innovation pipeline. From experimental workflows to simulation pipelines, metadata creation, and integration into knowledge graphs, the ideas taught in this course are meant to be used in real scientific work.

This section showcases concrete demonstrations and applications of the Five-Star approach. These examples illustrate how open formats, semantic annotations, and linked data workflows can:

- streamline laboratory practices,
- improve the quality and reusability of datasets,
- connect measurements, models, and metadata,
- and enable better automation and machine reasoning.

The following videos are embedded directly for convenience. They provide short, practical examples of how the principles discussed in the MOOC translate into real research scenarios.
