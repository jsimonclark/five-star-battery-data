Discussion#

The Five-Star Battery Data framework is not just a technical concept—it is a community effort. Its impact grows as more researchers adopt open practices, share metadata-rich datasets, and collaborate across institutions and disciplines.

This section provides a space for reflection and discussion. Here we highlight the experiences of students, researchers, and practitioners who have applied the Five-Star Battery Data principles in real projects. Their insights help illustrate:

  • what aspects of the framework were most valuable,

  • where they encountered challenges,

  • how the transition to open, structured, linked data changed their workflow,

  • and what opportunities they see for the future of battery data.
