Star One: Open Access
==========================================

.. contents:: Module outline
   :depth: 1
   :local:

Learning objectives
-------------------
- Publish your dataset with a clear, permissive license.
- Place the data in a trusted, citable repository (e.g., Zenodo).
- Capture minimal metadata so others can discover and cite your work.
